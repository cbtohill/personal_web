Welcome!

My name is Clár-Bríd (CB) Tohill and I am a PhD student at the University of Nottingham under the supervision of Dr Steven Bamford and Professor Christopher Conselice. My research focuses on galaxy morphology and evolution with machine learning. My current work is focused on applying unsupervised machine learning techniques to understand and classify the morphology of high redshift galaxies.
